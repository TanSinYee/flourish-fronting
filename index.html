About Us

Since 2000, our team members, Amber,Lynne and Agnes decide to start up our brand. Our first encounter took place at a humble dessert stall. Sharing similar tastes and exhanging ideas through conversations, we instantly clicked and decided to open a dessert shop together, which is Flourish & Frosting.

Our Concept

"Flourish" stands for vigorous growth and abundance and "Frosting" is the decoration of desserts, meaning beauty and nourishment.

FAQ

1. Do you offer delivery services ? Which area do you cover?

Yes, we have offer delivery services. We only deliver within Kuala Lumpur and Selangor areas.

2. What are your business hour ?

Our business hour from 8.00am to 8.00pm on weekday. For weekend, our business hour from 10.00am to 10.00pm. We are closed every Wednesday.

3. How is the quality of the ingredients in your shop ?

The ingredients we use are all bought on the same day to ensure freshness.

4. Do you provide pre-order services for cake ?

Yes, our shop is provide pre-order services for our cake, You can contact us directly or walk-in in business hour.

5. Is your shop provide refund services?

Yes, if the order cancelled within 24 hours which is ordered online.
